    Skogafoss

    Along the Southern Coast of Iceland lies one of the most magnificent water features in all of Europe. Skogafoss presents a unique experience to nature lovers in that it is viewable from both the base of the falls as well as elevation. A short but steep hike from the base of the falls leads up a hundred yard stair path ending in a can't miss outlook over the edge of Skogafoss.
